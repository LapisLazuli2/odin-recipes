Oyakodon

Description

Ingredients

  • 50g chicken thigh
  • 50g onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 stalks chopped mitsuba parsley
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 150g steamed rice

Broth

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 2 tablespoons dashi stock
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth in a pan. Combine the soy sauce, mirin, dashi stock and sugar. Stir to mix and dissolve the sugar.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the broth.
  3. Lightly beat the eggs. Avoid overmixing to create a soft and silky texture.
  4. Cut the onion into slices.
  5. Add the onion to the chicken, cover pan with lid and put it on medium heat.
  6. When the meat begins to turn white, flip it over.
  7. When the chicken is almost cooked, add the mitsuba parsley. Then, distribute the beaten egg.
  8. Cover again. Cook the egg at least halfway through and then adjust the consistency to your taste.
  9. Place the ingredients onto a bowl of hot steamed rice. Make a shallow hole in the center. And place the fresh egg yolk into it.
